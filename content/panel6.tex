 \paneltitle{OTHER VARIANT RULES}
\begin{scifitablebox}[screenbox,title=\textcolor{PanelGlow}{Paragon \& Renegade}]
This is a variant rule. You can play without it.
\begin{itemize}
  \item Give points based on choice and in groupings of 1-5. (More for major story beats.)
  \item A skill check can be augmented by P/R if you think it makes sense. They gain +1 for every 10 points they have of the appropriate stat.
\end{itemize}
\end{scifitablebox}
